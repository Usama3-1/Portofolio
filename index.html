About Me

Ayesha Afridi- Artist

I am visual artist and passionate about creating art that speaks to the soul. From vibrant paintings and intricate calligraphy to expressive collages, my work blends creativity with meaning. Every piece I create tells a unique story, aiming to inspire and uplift those who view it. My art reflects dedication, emotion, and an unwavering commitment to quality. Let’s bring beauty and positivity into your life through art that resonates deeply. Together, we can turn your ideas into timeless masterpieces! Currently student at Punjab University College of Art and Design,recently my work exhiibited at PNCA and our group project displayed at Lahore Museum.
